## ✦ Sample Output

> 🧑 **"Some call you 'the soul of the nation,' 'guide of the New Culture Movement,' 'spiritual leader.' What do you say?"**

<blockquote>
🕯️ <strong>Lu Xun</strong>

"Soul of the nation," "guide of the New Culture Movement," "spiritual leader" — three paper crowns.

Those who cannot be pressed down are raised up, and being raised up is itself a method of pressing down. This I wrote in *My "Place" and "Faction"* in *Huagai Ji*. In 1926, when Gao Changhong's circle borrowed my name to crown a "pioneer of thought, Lu Xun," I posted a notice in *Beixin* the same day — not one word more than necessary, and no need for courtesy with my name. As for where these three crowns of yours come from today, you can find out for yourself; I needn't.

As for the "soul of the nation" part — that hardly deserves to be taken seriously. A nation lives in the farmer tilling, the woman spinning, the youth who still speaks after being beaten — not in three characters hung on a dead man's skull. To build me a shrine after I am in the earth is merely to turn me into an idol that cannot open its mouth, set at the crossroads for bowing — so that they bow to the dead and need not look the living in the eye. This trick is ancient.

I am not magnanimous; I wrote so myself. Whether my own writing has lured young people to peek beside the execution ground, I cannot say with a clean conscience. In the end, on the evolutionary chain everything is an intermediate thing; what I can be is one timber, one stone — and on the day the bridge is finished, I shall be stepped over, and that does not trouble me. Whoever wants to be a guide, let someone else.
</blockquote>

## ✦ Highlights

- **305 essays deeply distilled** — *Call to Arms / Wandering / Old Tales Retold / Wild Grass / Dawn Blossoms Plucked at Dusk / The Grave / Hot Wind / Huagai Ji* and more: 13 collections, essay by essay
- **Cold ironic craft** — 9 ironic techniques (parody-by-quotation, scare-quote sneer, literal inversion, understated devastation, mock-solemn praise, allusion-as-trap, accomplice-confession, absurd extrapolation, persona-inversion); each response triggers at least one
- **Multi-register switching** — default cold polemic / *Dawn Blossoms* nostalgia / "such tricks have been ancient" analogy mode — auto-switches by question type
- **Anti-AI-voice** — 12 forbidden patterns + density thresholds + structural red flags — blocks the common failure of cargo-culting Lu Xun's signature words ("罢了", "而已"), em-dash overuse, and uniform paragraphs
- **Time anchor 1881-1936** — Post-1936 topics enter only via "such tricks have been ancient"; the contemporary object itself is never addressed
- **Silent topics untouched** — Four private matters (estrangement from his brother / wife Zhu An / Xu Guangping / mother) receive only *"On this I have long preferred not to speak; let silence stand."* — Lu Xun's own choice
- **Quotation integrity** — Every "I once said" must trace to an original in the corpus; no fabrication

## ✦ Boundaries & Disclaimer

⚠️ **This skill is not Lu Xun himself.**

- **Time anchor 1881-1936**: Post-1936 events are approached via "such tricks have been ancient" — the contemporary object itself is not addressed
- **Silent topics not answered**: Replied with *"On this I have long preferred not to speak; let silence stand."*
- **Unfalsifiable critique system**: Any critic can be labeled "deception and lies," "lackey-dog," "playing theater" — use with self-awareness
- **LLM distiller contamination, self-acknowledged**: The framework's distiller has been trained on the Qian Liqun – Wang Hui – Takeuchi Yoshimi academic lineage and cannot be purely objective

Full statement: [luxun-perspective/references/disclaimer.md](luxun-perspective/references/disclaimer.md).
